Business Transformation · Security
ADT
Challenge

ADT, a 150-year-old security leader, faced existential disruption from DIY smart home brands and Big Tech entrants commoditizing core monitoring products. Subscriber growth had stalled, acquisition costs were climbing, and the board needed a clear pivot strategy.

Action

Nexus Partners led a full-spectrum transformation across four parallel workstreams: reframing ADT's value proposition around the connected home category; designing the ADT+ ecosystem with AI-powered threat detection; structuring the strategic partnership framework that secured Google's $450M investment; and redesigning the go-to-market model with a direct digital channel to improve unit economics.

Result

Within 24 months, ADT repositioned from a commoditized monitoring provider to an integrated smart home ecosystem company commanding premium pricing and renewed investor confidence.

Digital Transformation · Entertainment
Sony
Challenge

Sony's entertainment portfolio — music, film, gaming, and streaming — operated as siloed businesses with separate technology stacks and customer identity systems. Despite owning the world's most valuable IP, Sony failed to capture the cross-category subscriber value that integrated platforms like Apple and Amazon were generating.

Action

We designed a unified digital ecosystem strategy: established a single customer record spanning PlayStation, Sony Music, and Sony Pictures; designed the Sony One cross-platform membership; deployed shared data infrastructure enabling personalization at scale; and aligned 300+ stakeholders across four business units in three continents.

Result

Sony's entertainment divisions now share a common customer data layer and cross-sell architecture that has materially increased lifetime value and reduced churn across the entire portfolio.

Digital Transformation · Consumer
Hallmark
Challenge

Hallmark faced fifteen consecutive years of physical card sales decline as digital communication displaced paper correspondence. Despite deep brand affinity, the company lacked a credible digital revenue model after several failed attempts at the digital gifting space.

Action

We designed and launched Hallmark+, a subscription platform with unlimited digital cards and a physical mailing service; built an AI-powered personalization engine; launched the Hallmark Moments AR mobile app; and restructured the product organization to compress time-to-market from 14 months to under 90 days.

Result

Hallmark established itself as a digital-first greeting platform while maintaining the emotional brand positioning that defines category leadership, creating a durable recurring revenue engine.
